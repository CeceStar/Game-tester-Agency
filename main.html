Let us help you develop your game!

Do you have an idea for a new game, but need testers? You come to the right place! Board games of all their sort, dice games, card games, or digital games like FPS, MOBA, Tower defence - you name it! Talk to us and we will help you with proper feedback and documentation, so you can develop your idea and create that dream. Stop bothering your friends and family, we will help you instead with enthusiasm, excitement and engagement!

We don't care what type of game you have created. Is it an easy-peasy game that takes about 10 minutes to finish? Is it an 4 hour long sessions including complicated classes, dices or just rules that you really need to keep track on? Doesn't matter to us!

Our testers age are ranging from 3 years old up to 80 years, so we have some one for you! Our testers feel that they can never be to young or to old for playing games, it's all a mindset.

Are your targeting gaming wizards that are use to playing alot of games? Or do your want to see how complete newbies in gaming would play your games? Don't your worry about that, we got you!

The test will be well documented. We will make sure that you get all the documentation you need, both the procedure and general feedback regarding the test.
